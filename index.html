About me

I am currently studying for my Master's degree specialized in Medical Image Analysis in an Erasmus Mundus Joint Master Degree coordinated by the University of Girona.

My main research interest covers the deep learning topics, weakly/semi/self supervised learning with focus on generalization/domain adaption applied to breast and fetal imaging.

I received my Bachelor's degree in computer engineering from Cairo University in 2021. My thesis "Tracking Multiple People Over a Network of Cameras, A Contact Tracing Perspective" was supervised by Dr. AbdelMoneim Bayoumi with an additional industrial mentorship from DELL Technologies. Throughout the degree, I got the chance to do a summer internship at AIIT, Japan with research funding from the Ministry of Higher Education, as well as an undergraduate research position at the AIRe Research Group, University of Bradford, UK under the supervision of Daniel Neagu and Amr Abdullatif, working on COVID-19 time forecasting with deep learning models.
